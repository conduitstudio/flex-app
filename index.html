Agile Studio

Designed to adapt quickly for rapidly
changing team activities.

Sprint Planning

  • Sprints are about moving fast and
    iterating
  • Work needs to be visible to help teams plan projects, define roles and get started quickly

Working in Pairs

  • Enables teammates to shift from individual
    tasks to working side-by-side
  • Often important for coding or co-creation

Daily Stand-Up

  • Space quickly adapts for regular meetings
    that keep work and activities visible to the
    team
  • Helps you stand up, meet and get to work

Creative Studio

Helps teams immerse and focus - as a group
and individuals - to generate new ideas.

Workshop

  • Allows the display of information
    to surround the team
  • Helps teams scope work and start
    projects

Brainstorm

  • Movement helps teams generate more
    and better ideas
  • Fosters a range of postures and
    behaviors - standing, perching, drawing

    from stakeholders

Focus Retreat

    and teamwork

Mobile Power

Mobile Power allows teams and individuals to stay powered up throughout the day while providing the freedom to work anywhere they want.
  • Lightweight and portable to take with you wherever you go
  • Powers up to 4 devices like laptops or phones via three (3) USB-C and (1) USB-A charging ports
  • Re-charges in less than eight hours on proprietary charging tray
